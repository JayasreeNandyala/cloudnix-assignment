Step 2 — Product type & category

Add the product type and categories that describe your shop. Add Platforms if you support apps.

Why this matters

Providing correct product types and categories helps customers find your products faster and improves search & recommendations.

Example
Product Type: Electronics
Category: Mobile Phones
Tip: You can add sub-categories later when adding individual products.
